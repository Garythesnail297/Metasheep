METASHEEP
5k unique collectible adorable Sheep, stored on the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 standard tokens, being compatible with all exchanges. Metasheep are “Non-Fungible Tokens”, meaning no central entity can take them away from you.

Despite this standardization this collection has an unique twist ; )
ABOUT THE PROJECT
Metasheep are an up and coming NFT collection with a twist.
Each Sheep is unique, generated through over 100 hand drawn traits.
Owning a Metasheep allows you to partake in exclusive games and events with Ethereum prizes, derived through a percentage of the minting pool.
ROADMAP

Launch Project

Metasheep’s Twitter has been released and we are building a community that believes in the project! Also, during this time Garythesnail will reveal some collabs for the project.

Discord and whitelist open

When the Twitter gets to 5000 followers, the discord will be opened and the first 100 to join it get an instant whitelist. Then, whitelists will be given throughout the community in challenges or to people that are active and believe in the project! The Discord will have mods which will pick people who deserve a whitelist in the project!

Presale

When the whitelist is full, the presale date will be announced! Anyone who is whitelisted will be able to mint an amount of sheeps that will be revealed during this phase. The mint price will also be decided by this point.

Public mint

All the sheeps remaining after the presale will be released for public mint where people can buy them for the same price as in presale. The public mint will be exactly 24 hours after the presale.

Challenges start

Anyone who owns a Sheep will be instantly registered to ETH giveaways! Also, the NFT will act as a ticket letting you enter challenges with the community to win prizes such as ETH, whitelist or even NFTs!
